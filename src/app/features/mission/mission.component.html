Mission

Bitcoin Education
built differenly
Simple. Interactive.

If you've tried to learn about Bitcoin, you know it can be confusing and overwhelming. There's just so much to know, and most of discussions out there assume a ton of prior knowledge and context.
Bitcoin education needs a more interactive and engaging platform, particularly for newcomers to the space, ensuring they don't feel overwhelmed about where to begin.

I'm crafting a platform rich with animations, drawings and hands-on lab to break down complex Bitcoin topics into simpler terms.
The big goal is to build a bridge between experienced developer and newcomers facilitating and encouraging for more dialogue and discussion between them.
